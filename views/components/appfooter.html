Application Footer

The Application Footer appears on every single pages. Things generally see in the footer are copyright information, contact information, Privacy policy, terms of use, press release information, call-to-actions and sitemap links.

Overview
When to Use:
  • The global footer should be used on every standard pages at the bottom.
  • The footer should not be too information heavy, and it is advisable to keep the design simple and not overflowing with graphics.
Single Line Footer

The single line footer provides minimal content and links to pages of interests where it's divided into 2 columns.
