Step Indicator

A step indicator communicates to the user the progress of a particular screen flow.

Overview

asdf

When to Use:

  • When the user needs to complete a complicated task or a series of tasks or decisions affecting downstream choices (e.g. connect bank account or when onboarding a client)
  • When the user needs to fill out a long form. A step flow will break it down into several digestible sections (e.g. create a new employee, sign up payment)
Basic

The basic step indicator contains default styles for active, incomplete and disabled states. To link a step use an <a> tag.

Example Markup:



            
Fluid

            
Step indicators can can be be fluid or flex.

            


            
Size

            
Supports various sized step bars.

            


            
Colors

            
Colors can be set according to the background color system.

            

            

            

            

            

            

            

            

            

            


            
Icons

            
Step indicators support icons to the left of step content.

            


            
Descriptions

            
Descriptions can support indicator labels and are place underneath.

            


            
Numbered

            
Steps may be numbered and once complete will display a checkmark icon.

            


            
Orientation

            
In addition to horizonal display, indicators may be positioned vertically.

            


            
Responsive

            
Both horizontal and vertial indicators are responsive for phones. Horizontal indicators may be set vertically for tablet.

            


            
Placement

            
Indicators can be placed on the top or bottom container.

            

            
            
Grid

            
Steps will automatically fill the avaialble space equally.

            


            
Classes

            

                
                    

                        Class
                        Description
                    

                
                
                    

                        .steps
                        Indicates placement for step indicators and adds default styling to the container.
                    

                    

                        .steps-numbered
                        Adds sequential numbering to the steps
                    

                    

                        .steps-vertical
                        Orients the steps vertically
                    

                    

                        .steps-fluid
                        Makes step bar full width of the parent container.
                    

                    

                        .steps-top
                        Attached steps to the top of a container
                    

                    

                        .steps-bottom
                        Attached steps to the bottom of a container
                    

                    

                        .steps-sm
                        Sets the height to .8rem
                    

                    

                        .steps-lg
                        Sets the height to 1.2rem
                    

                    

                        .steps-noflex
                        Remove flexing from the steps. Steps will align left
                    

                    

                        .bg-*
                        Use modifier classes to change the appearance of each step by adding .bg-* to the .steps class. You may also apply variations by adding .dk, .dker, .lt, .lter to darken or lighten the base color.
                    

                    

                        .step
                        Applies default stying to each step
                    

                    

                        .step-body
                        Indicates placement for step content
                    

                    

                        .step-title
                        Applies styling to a step title
                    

                    

                        .step-description
                        Applies styling to a step description
                    

                    

                        .step-icon
                        Displays and icon to the left of step content
                    

                    

                        .active
                        Applies highlight styles
                    

                    

                        .complete
                        Applies checkmark to a completed step
                    

                    

                        .disabled
                        Disables cursor and dims step indicator.
